Generated Charts

This folder contains charts generated by the Python visualisation script.

Purpose
The charts provide visual summaries of key business insights derived from SQL analysis.

Charts Included
- `profit_by_region.png`  
  Comparison of profit across regions.

- `monthly_sales_trend.png`  
  Time-series view of monthly sales performance.

- `monthly_profit_trend.png`  
  Time-series view of monthly profit performance.

- `bottom_10_subcategories_profit.png`  
  Identification of loss-making product sub-categories.

- `top_customers_by_profit.png`  
  Profit contribution of the top 10 customers.

Notes
All charts are generated programmatically from SQL-derived CSVs to ensure consistency and reproducibility.
